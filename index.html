Who We Are

Asia's Largest Timber Market's Premium Expert

Located in the heart of WHS Timber Market, Kirti Nagar — Asia's largest wood hub — K K Moulding is a specialized manufacturer and supplier of top-tier wooden interior components.

With over 20 years of expertise, led by Mr. Subodh Prasad, we provide tailored wood solutions for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects across Delhi NCR. Our shop is conveniently located near Hanuman Mandir.

From high-finish Teak moldings to specialized Jali doors and robust Chaukhat, our commitment to quality has made us a trusted name for architects and homeowners alike.
